About WDC

Purpose

The purpose of the Web Development Club is as follows:

  • To promote learning and professional growth in the field of web development.
  • To establish a network of like-minded individuals who want to pursue web development.
  • To give back to the Iowa State University community and local community by offering web development services to students, faculty, and clubs and organizations.
  • To give back to the greater web development community by writing and posting public code libraries, extensions, plugins, etc.
